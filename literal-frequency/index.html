Literal Frequency — reading books as data

Literal Frequency builds data visualisations live from the Source Library, the open-access archive of digitised, translated books. It loads a book over the API and reads its word frequency.

The same vocabulary is shown four ways: a typographic word cloud, an animated glowing word nebula, a packed bubble field, and a ranked bar chart.

Every view links back to its source text on sourcelibrary.org. Switch books, choose the translation or the original OCR, and set how many pages to analyse to explore the collection.
