Generatives — a generative-visual lab

Generatives is a live lab of animated, embeddable visual treatments for the visual language of a quantum-computing futures project. Flow fields, layered noise, wave interference, agent systems, slime-mould networks and reaction-diffusion all run in real time in the browser.

Every treatment is tunable: shape it with its own parameters plus the universal complexity and chaos knobs, recolour it with the background, low and high colour pickers, then reseed or randomise for endless variations.

Any treatment can be lifted out as a self-contained, copy-paste embed and dropped into a banner, hero section or web page, or exported as an MP4 at any size.

The gallery includes Domain Warp, Plasma Flow, Voronoi Cells, Wave Interference, Physarum (the Jones agent model), Boids, Field Dynamics, Organic Turbulence and Reaction Diffusion.
