Hyperscale — build an AI data center in 3D

Hyperscale is a small 3D builder about the physical reality behind the compute boom. You drop buildings on a green terrain plot — server halls, GPU pods, cooling towers, substations — and keep four things in balance: power, heat, compute and cash.

Substations supply megawatts; server halls and GPU pods turn power into compute and heat; cooling towers vent the heat as steam. Underfeed power or fall behind on cooling and machines throttle, glow red, then fail and go dark until you repair them. Demand for compute climbs every day, so standing still means falling behind.

Orbit the camera, watch the fans spin and the towers steam, and grow the site. Runs are seeded — heat waves, power-price spikes, grid outages and demand surges replay identically for a given seed.
