Hyperscale — run an AI data center

Hyperscale is a small management sim about the physical reality behind the compute boom. You lay out a data-center floor — server racks, substations, cooling units — and keep four things in balance: power, heat, compute and cash.

Every rack draws power and throws off heat; substations supply megawatts; cooling units pull the heat back out. Underfeed power or fall behind on cooling and racks throttle, then degrade, then fail. Meanwhile market demand for compute climbs day after day, so standing still means falling behind.

Runs are seeded, so the same seed plays out the same way. Heat waves, power-price spikes, grid outages and demand surges keep each run honest. Stay solvent, keep the floor cool, and serve as much compute as the market will take.
