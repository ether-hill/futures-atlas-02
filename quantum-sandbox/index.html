Quantum Sandbox — drawing quantum systems

Quantum Sandbox is a prototyping dashboard for quantum-computing generative systems, where every amplitude is drawn as colour — magnitude as density and phase as hue.

It spans systems including a Schrödinger split-step Fourier solver with double-slit and tunnelling, 2D quantum walks, Chladni eigenmodes and nodal lines, Grover amplitude amplification, and Bloch and Majorana qubit geometry.

Tune the system, seed and parameters in real time, then capture stills as PNGs or record a clip.
